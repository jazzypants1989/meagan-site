What You Need to Do

📍 Define Your Foundation

Set your main goal
Write down ONE specific outcome (e.g., "Get 50 qualified leads per month")
Define your ideal clients
List 2-3 specific client types and their biggest pain points
Craft your value proposition
One clear sentence: what you do, for whom, and why it matters
Choose primary & backup actions
Main CTA (book consultation) + backup (download guide, subscribe)

✍️ Create Your Content

Write homepage copy
Headline, subheadline, intro paragraph, and 2 key benefit points
Create service page content
For each service: problem it solves, your solution, client benefit
Gather social proof
Collect 3-5 client testimonials, case studies, or results with names
Write call-to-action text
Exact button text for every form ("Book Strategy Call," "Get Proposal")

🎯 Plan Your Launch

Define what to track
List exact actions: form submits, phone clicks, email signups
Write launch announcements
Draft email, LinkedIn post, and any ads to promote your new site
Plan ongoing updates
Decide who updates content, adds case studies, writes blog posts

What to Delegate

🎨 Design & Branding

Provide brand assets
Logo files, brand colors, fonts, and style preferences
Supply photography
Professional headshot, client photos, or approved stock images
Provide visual extras
Icons, diagrams, process flows, or videos you want included

⚙️ Technical Setup

Website development
Build responsive site with your content and branding
Analytics integration
Set up Google Analytics, Tag Manager, and tracking pixels
Conversion tracking
Configure form submissions, click-to-call, and goal tracking
Legal pages setup
Add privacy policy, terms of service, and required disclaimers
Performance optimization
Optimize for speed, mobile responsiveness, and SEO basics
Domain & hosting setup
Configure domain, SSL certificate, and reliable hosting
Testing & launch
Cross-browser testing, form testing, and go-live coordination
